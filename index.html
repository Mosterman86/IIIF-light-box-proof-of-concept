About this tool
The Lightbox IIIF Builder lets curators and educators quickly assemble a visual workspace from any IIIF-compliant collection. Paste manifest URLs into the slots, name your sections, then share the result as a single URL — no accounts, no uploads, no server required. Images are loaded directly from their source institutions via OpenSeadragon 4.1 and the IIIF Presentation API (v2 & v3).
How to share
Click ↗ Share Lightbox to copy a URL to your clipboard. The URL encodes all your manifest URLs and section titles — anyone who opens it will see the same lightbox in View Mode, ready to explore. Recipients can switch to Build Mode using the toggle in the header to edit or remix the lightbox further.
