IIIF Lightbox — Builder & Viewer

Creating a Lightbox

Paste a IIIF manifest URL into any slot to load an image. Name your sections, add a description, and fill your lightbox with up to 16 objects — then click Share Lightbox to copy a shareable link.

Viewing a Shared Lightbox

Open a shared link and all images load instantly. Clear individual viewers using the × button in each toolbar, or use Clear All at the top of the page to reset everything — then fill the lightboxes with your own images and share a new link.

How to share
Click the Share Lightbox button at the top of the page to copy a URL to your clipboard. The link encodes all manifest URLs, section titles, and descriptions — anyone who opens it sees the same lightbox, fully loaded.
About this tool
A curatorial and educational tool for assembling IIIF images from any IIIF-compliant collection into a shareable visual workspace. No accounts, uploads, or server required. Images render via OpenSeadragon 4.1 and the IIIF Presentation API (v2 & v3).
